Wakeup story

I was going out towards uncertainty. Suddenly I stopped in front of a house in the darkness of night. I laid down on the floor of the mud house. I wake up hearing the chirping of birds. I saw the golden ray of sunlight making bath the ground of the veranda. The leaves and flowers decorated themselves with a festive look. Breathing comfortably I saw a few bright white dressed people around me. Two of them came closer and said, today you goanna die. Please come back after taking bath in that pond. Then they took me into the same veranda after making me wear like them. They indicated I go to the very last door and lay down on the ground after closing the door. It was a very sweet smell to feel. Water sprayed on the ground just a few times back. It was a very peaceful and cool atmosphere. I was thinking in front of that door, is there any work rest to do? I realize whatever I wanted to do, I did already, and whatever is left has been started already. The smell of flowers was no more and birds stopped chirping when I entered that room and close the door. My body felt paralyzed. Suddenly I woke up and felt awesome. The dream of last night inspired me to walk again to infinity.
