About me

Hi, this is Titu Debnath I am an Artist from Bangladesh. I was born in a lower-middle-class farmer family in the Comilla district of the Chittagong division in 1985. I was involved in all kinds of social activities starting from agricultural work to cultural activities in rural life. After completing my school and college education in the village, admitted to the Department of Drawing and painting in the Faculty of Fine Arts, at the University of Dhaka in 2005. I have been involved in integrated and solo exhibitions from the time of my studies to the present time. In 2015 successfully arranged my first solo exhibition. In the same year, join in the game industry for learning industrial art. Now I have been creating and developing ideas from out of nowhere into reality, Creative writing or scripting for production, AAA and Hollywood category High-end assets developing also Direction for Film and Game is my key responsibility for the industry. To build the world from imagination to reality with maintaining industry-standard needs I had to analyze various methods and research to find out the very best problem-solving way. I constantly do Explore Form, Shape, Color, Light, Shadow, in a Contemporary context from mother nature and her wings. There is a Huge Amount of Philosophical movement, Science, Modern, Ancient Architecture, Post-Impressionism, Modern, DADA, Bauhaus, Contemporary, and many more known or unknown things provoking me to build things in my mind for Production. And the most important thing for me I believe is I am still learning every day like a fresher.
